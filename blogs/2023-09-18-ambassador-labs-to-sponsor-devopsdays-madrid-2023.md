<h4>Community Events</h4><p>We are excited to announce that we’re sponsoring the upcoming developer event <a href="https://devopsdays.org/events/2023-madrid/welcome/">DevOpsDays in Madrid</a> on October 6, 2023. First held in Ghent, Belgium, in 2009, DevOps Days has grown in popularity and has expanded internationally, with 51 events worldwide in 2022 and 60 events in 2023. The Madrid event, which was <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?app=desktop&amp;v=ZYk772mNBH0">last held in 2020</a>, will welcome 500+ developers and other tech professionals to the CEU San Pablo Monteprincipe University campus.</p><figure><img alt="" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1024/1*b6RNSjbqYuJMsGIN_SaYpg.png" /></figure><h4><strong>What is DevOpsDays?</strong></h4><p>DevOpsDays is a user-oriented technical conference covering topics related to software development and IT infrastructure operations. As many developers find themselves managing the creation of software as well as its rollout, monitoring, observation, and maintenance, DevOpsDays focuses on how these different concerns interact in practice.</p><p>The conference is organized and run by volunteers from the community. One of the organizers is Xavier Portilla Edo, Infrastructure Team Lead at Voiceflow, who is also a second-year <a href="https://www.getambassador.io/ambassador-community-advocates">Ambassador Community Advocate</a>.
